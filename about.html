About Me

I'm a freelance journalist covering underground music, internet culture, and local stories that matter. My work ranges from artist interviews and scene reporting to long-form features, with a focus on voice, honesty, and the details that give subcultures their shape.

In my free time, I listen to underground rap and get lost in internet culture and current events.
